About Me

A Little About Me:

I was born on the east coast but raised, for my formative years, in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. After graduating from Carnegie Mellon University with my bachelors in civil engineering, I pursued a career in the field of nuclear engineering. After a few years, I transitioned into working with our new engineers and found my calling to revamp our new hire training program and then transitioned to managing our engineering recruiting program. I've been enjoying this work and haven't really looked back to consider my work options until this past fall. After some soul searching, I realized I wanted more flexibility in my work schedule and more day-to-day problem solving, and, voila! I began my journey to becoming a fullstack web developer. If you want to follow my journey and see how far I progress, be sure to favorite this portfolio link. Also, check back in to join my mailing list! Once I have the database and Javascript running, I'll be able to send out updates on my finished projects and solicit input and requests for other projects that I should work on.

If you want to know more about my background, check out my resume or visit the contact me section below! TTYL.
